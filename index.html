What's the best part of your favorite song? Is is the first beat of music, when you realize what's playing? You know...the moment your ears perk up and you await the first line.

Is it the first verse, when you close your eyes and begin to sing along with the lyrics you know by heart.

Or is it the familiarity of the chorus, the lines that so often get wedged in your brain, leaving you humming well-known words and melodies for hours?

Could it be the bridge, where new harmonies and rythyms emerge?

Or the final moments of the song that make you want to reach for the repeat button and hear it all over again?

Three years ago I married my favorite, and it's impossible to figure out the best part.
